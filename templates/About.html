About Mzanzi Market

Celebrating South Africa's vibrant market culture since 2025.

Who we are

Mzanzi Market is an online community that connects people who love local markets—artisans, food producers, and customers alike.

Our mission is to keep South Africa’s market culture alive by giving vendors a digital stage and visitors an easy way to discover their next favourite stall.

Our Goals

Sustainability

Promote eco-friendly practices and conscious consumerism in market culture.

Community Support

Foster collaboration and shared resources among local creators.

Support Local Business

Help small vendors reach new customers and grow their brands.

Our Story

Established in 2025, Mzanzi Market began as a simple idea: create a digital space where the energy of South African markets could thrive online.

What started with a handful of stalls has grown into a community of hundreds of vendors and thousands of shoppers who believe in authentic, locally made products.

Today we continue to build technology that makes it easy for anyone to discover, visit and support local markets across the country.
