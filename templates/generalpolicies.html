Vendors are responsible for the quality and safety of their goods. The sale of illegal items, weapons, or stolen goods is strictly prohibited. Vendors must honor their commitment to attend markets they have registered for.

Mzanzi Market is a directory and booking platform. We are not liable for physical accidents at market events or disputes over transactions. We encourage all users to meet in public, safe market environments and inspect goods before purchasing.
