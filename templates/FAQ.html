Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mzanzi Market?

Mzanzi Market is a platform that connects market organizers, vendors, and customers in one place. You can find upcoming markets, join as a vendor, or explore local businesses and events near you.

How do I register as a vendor?

Vendors can sign up through the Vendor Community page. Once registered, youâ€™ll be able to apply to markets, set up stalls, and showcase your products to customers.

How do customers find upcoming markets?

Customers can visit the Upcoming Events page to see all scheduled markets, their locations, and event details. You can also view vendor lists and times for each market.

Is there a fee for joining Mzanzi Market?

Creating a customer account is free. Vendors and organizers may have small listing or participation fees depending on the event type.

Can I contact organizers directly?

Yes! Each market listing includes organizer contact details so vendors and customers can reach out for additional information or collaboration.
